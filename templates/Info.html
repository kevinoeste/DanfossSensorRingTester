Preparing fixture for test:

Before starting the test, a technician must check that the hardware is plugged in, the tracks are free from debris,
and has no obstructions in the path. Then a sensor ring can be placed in the mount for testing.

Initializing test on application:

To perform a test click on the start test tab. Enter the serial number of the sensor ring and then click start test.
The test will begin and once complete the results will be displayed along with if it passed or failed.
